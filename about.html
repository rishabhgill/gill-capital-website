About Gill Capital

I’m an internationally certified business coach with extensive experience in founder psychology. Over the years, I’ve seen that while many advisors teach marketing, sales, operations, and finance, they often overlook the most critical factor: the founder’s mindset.

At Gill Capital, we prioritize mental clarity, awareness of biases, and a growth-oriented belief system to help founders transform their businesses into industry leaders. Our goal is to empower startups through capital investment, strategic mentorship, and a deep commitment to founder psychology.

Investment Thesis

We invest in visionary founders who recognize that personal growth and business growth are inseparable. By combining capital infusion with one-on-one coaching, we help founders overcome mental barriers and chart a path to exponential growth.
