Book a Pitch Session

Our pitch sessions focus on Problem, Solution, and Traction in the first meeting. We then conduct deeper dives into each aspect of your business, including psychometric tests to understand how founders make decisions under pressure.

By booking a pitch session with Gill Capital, you’ll receive:

  • Direct feedback on your business model
  • Insights into founder mindset and areas for growth
  • Potential for funding and long-term partnership

We encourage you to come prepared with a concise presentation and any supporting documents that highlight your product, traction, and vision. Successful pitches will move on to more in-depth discussions.
