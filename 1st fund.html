The First 10Cr Fund

Our inaugural 10Cr fund is designed to back product-ready to revenue-stage companies poised for exponential growth. We aim to help them reach Series D and beyond, driving strong returns for our investors.

  • Stage: Product-ready to revenue
  • Sectors: Education, Health, AI, Finance, and Aerospace
  • Founder Qualities: Mindful, competent, honest, and open to coaching

Our goal is to deliver a minimum of 10x returns within five years. Even for startups that don’t make it to scale, we aim for at least a 10x exit, ensuring robust investor returns.
